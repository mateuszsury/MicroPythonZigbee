# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, professional, and harassment-free
collaboration environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and technical decisions.
- Assume good intent, then clarify with facts.
- Accept and apply valid technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Deliberate disruption, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Repeated bad-faith argumentation after clear moderation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and
other contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer via GitHub contact channels.
All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.
